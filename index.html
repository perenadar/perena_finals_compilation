A resume is a formal document that provides an overview of a person's professional qualifications, including their work experience, education, skills, and accomplishments. It is typically used when applying for jobs.

A registration form is a document or digital interface used to collect information from individuals for the purpose of enrolling them in a service, event, or system. It typically gathers personal and contact details.

A restaurant menu is a comprehensive list of food and beverage offerings available for customers at a restaurant. It serves as a key communication tool between the restaurant and its patrons, providing details about the dishes.
An online shop, also known as an e-commerce store, is a website or digital platform where consumers can browse, select, and purchase products or services over the internet.
